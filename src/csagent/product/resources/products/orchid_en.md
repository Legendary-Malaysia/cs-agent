- Orchid has been upgraded from 20% to 25% fragrance oil  
- Higher concentration = longer-lasting scent  
- Designed for customers who want noticeable performance without heaviness  

**Key Message for Customers:**  
“Orchid now lasts longer on skin and clothing with a richer scent experience.”

### 4.2 Updated Formula & Natural Colour Change  
- Increased fragrance oil may result in a slight yellow tint

- Colour change does not affect quality or performance  

**Sales Tip:**  
Reassure customers confidently:  
“The colour change shows the formula is richer and more concentrated—it’s a sign of enhancement, not a defect.”
