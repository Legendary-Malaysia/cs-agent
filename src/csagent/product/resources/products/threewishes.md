## 1. Product Overview  
Three Wishes is a luxurious 3-in-1 self-care fragrance collection, created to support a journey of self-love, awareness, and fulfillment.  

Unlike conventional perfumes, Three Wishes is non-alcoholic, water-based, and skin-friendly, making it suitable for daily use on skin, hair, and clothes.  

It combines emotional storytelling with functional benefits—making it both a fragrance and a self-care ritual.  

**Core Concept:**  
Three Wishes represents three meaningful stages of self-love — beginning, awakening, and fulfillment.  

---

## 2. What Makes Three Wishes Special (Key Selling Pillars)  

### A. Non-Alcoholic & Skin-Friendly  
- 0% alcohol formulation  
- Gentle on sensitive skin  
- Does not dry out skin or irritate  
- Safe for daily, repeated use  

**Sales Tip:**  
Ideal for customers who avoid traditional perfumes due to dryness, irritation, or sensitivity.  

---

### B. Water-Based with Encapsulation Technology  
- Oil and water-based formula combined using nano-technology  
- Slower evaporation rate  
- Fragrance lasts longer throughout the day  
- Light, milky texture that feels luxurious, not heavy  

**Key Message to Customers:**  
“You enjoy a long-lasting scent without the heaviness of traditional perfumes.”  

---

### C. Moisturising & Nourishing  
Infused with skin-conditioning ingredients:  
- Glycerin for hydration  
- Butylene Glycol for smoothness  
- Coconut-derived ingredients for softness  

Leaves skin feeling moisturised and pampered.  

**Sales Tip:**  
Position it as a fragrance + skincare hybrid.  

---

### D. Versatile Use  
Safe for:  
- Skin  
- Hair  
- Clothes  

Will not stain fabric.  
Perfect for reapplication anytime, anywhere.  

**Example Line:**

## 6. Common Customer Questions & Suggested Responses  

**Q: Is it long-lasting even though it’s water-based?**  
Yes. Thanks to encapsulation technology, the fragrance evaporates more slowly and lasts longer than typical water-based perfumes.  

**Q: Can I spray it on my clothes?**  
Yes. It’s fabric-safe and won’t stain or damage clothing.  

**Q: Is it suitable for sensitive skin?**  
Yes. It is alcohol-free, moisturising, and formulated to be skin-friendly.
