## 1. Product Overview  
Spirit I is a thoughtfully curated perfume set featuring three distinct fragrances: **Hope, Love, and Confidence**. Each scent represents a core element of life and personal growth, allowing the wearer to express their mood, intention, or occasion through fragrance.  

**Core Philosophy**  
- Shine with Hope  
- Do with Love  
- Strive with Confidence  

Spirit I is designed for versatility, emotional connection, and everyday elegance — making it a meaningful fragrance set rather than a single-scent purchase.  

---

## 2. Target Customer Profile  
Spirit I appeals to customers who:  
- Enjoy variety instead of committing to one scent  
- Believe fragrance reflects emotion, mood, and identity  
- Want a perfume suitable for different occasions  
- Appreciate thoughtful gifting with symbolic meaning  
- Prefer elegant, modern scents that are easy to wear  

**Ideal for:**

## 3. Fragrance Breakdown & Storytelling  

### Hope  
**Mood:** Fresh, joyful, uplifting  
**Fragrance Profile:** Green Leaves, Floral  

**Scent Journey**  
- **Top notes:** Bellflower, Dewdrop, Cloves  
- **Heart notes:** Persimmon, Peach  
- **Base notes:** Musk, Rouge Powder  

**Sales Story Angle**  
Hope evokes the feeling of a fresh morning — light, airy, and optimistic. It’s perfect for starting the day with positivity and clarity. It settles into a warm, comforting embrace with Musk and Rouge Powder at its base, leaving a lingering sensation of hope.  

**Best For**  
- Daytime wear  
- Work or casual outings  
- Customers who prefer clean, fresh scents  

**Sales Tip (Example):**  
“This is the scent you wear when you want to feel refreshed and positive — like a fresh start every morning.”  

---

### Love  
**Mood:** Uplifting, sophisticated, romantic  
**Fragrance Profile:** Woody, Floral  

**Scent Journey**  
- **Top notes:** Cherry, Orange Blossom  
- **Heart notes:** Tulip, Jasmine  
- **Base notes:** Lily of the Valley, Musk  

**Sales Story Angle**  
Love is charming and elegant, with a romantic warmth that lingers softly. It creates an emotional connection without being overpowering. It leaves a lingering, mysterious trail of love.  

**Best For**  
- Dates and social occasions  
- Evening outings  
- Customers who enjoy soft floral elegance  

**Sales Tip (Example):**  
“This scent feels romantic and graceful — perfect for moments when you want to leave a gentle, unforgettable impression.”  

---

### Confidence  
**Mood:** Romantic, elegant, empowering  
**Fragrance Profile:** Floral, Fruity  

**Scent Journey**  
- **Top notes:** Quince, Grapefruit  
- **Heart notes:** Hyacinth, Jasmine  
- **Base notes:** Musk, Iris, Cedar  

**Sales Story Angle**  
Confidence is expressive and memorable. It’s designed for moments when you want to stand tall and feel empowered. Perfect for those who seek to make a memorable statement.  

**Best For**  
- Events or special occasions  
- Presentations or important meetings  
- Customers who want a statement scent  

**Sales Tip (Example):**  
“This is the fragrance you wear when you want to feel bold and self-assured — it makes a lasting impression.”  

### Packaging
Available as a gift sets: 3 × 15 ml bottles containing Hope, Love, and Confidence. Also available as a travel kit: 3 x 3ml bottles.
