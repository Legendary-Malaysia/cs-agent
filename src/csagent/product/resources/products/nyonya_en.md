## 1. Product Overview  
The Nyonya Series is a fragrance collection inspired by Malaysia’s rich Peranakan (Nyonya) heritage, blending cultural storytelling with modern perfumery. Each scent reflects a different aspect of Nyonya life — attire, cuisine, and traditions — resulting in a collection that feels nostalgic yet contemporary.  

**Core Idea for Sales**  
This is not just perfume — it is heritage captured in scent, designed for customers who value culture, craftsmanship, and meaningful stories behind what they wear.  

---

## 2. Key Selling Concept  
**"A Tribute to Nyonya Heritage"**  

When presenting the Nyonya Series, emphasize:  
- Cultural inspiration rooted in Malaysian identity  
- Elegant storytelling translated into wearable fragrances  
- A balance of tradition and modern sophistication

## 3. Collection Overview – Three Distinct Scents  

### A. Kebaya Blooms  
**Elegant • Floral • Feminine**  

**Scent Character**  
- Inspired by the floral motifs of traditional Nyonya kebaya  
- Soft, graceful, and timelessly elegant  

**Fragrance Notes**  
- **Top:** Ambrette, Leafy Notes, Lemon  
- **Heart:** Orange Blossom, Violet, Ylang Ylang  
- **Base:** Musk, Powdery Vetiver  

**Main Impression**  
- Floral and refined  
- Clean elegance with a gentle powdery finish  

**Ideal For**  
- Daytime wear  
- Office or formal settings  
- Customers who love classic, graceful floral scents  

**Sales Tip**  
"If you enjoy elegant florals that feel polished and timeless, Kebaya Blooms is perfect — it’s like wearing a beautifully embroidered kebaya in scent form."

---

### B. Nyonya Aromatic  
**Fresh • Spicy • Sophisticated**  

**Scent Character**  
- Inspired by herbs and spices found in traditional Nyonya cuisine  
- Warm, aromatic, and uplifting with citrus freshness  

**Fragrance Notes**  
- **Top:** Bergamot, Black Pepper, Lemongrass  
- **Heart:** Osmanthus, Rose, Violet  
- **Base:** Musk, Labdanum, Amber  

**Main Impression**  
- Citrus-floral with a spicy edge  
- Energetic yet refined  

**Ideal For**  
- Day-to-night transition  
- Customers who like fresh scents with character  
- Those who prefer something unique but not overpowering  

**Sales Tip**  
"This scent feels like stepping into a Nyonya kitchen — aromatic, lively, and full of character, yet still very elegant."  

---

### C. Ondeh Delights  
**Warm • Sweet • Comforting**  

**Scent Character**  
- Inspired by the beloved Nyonya dessert Ondeh-ondeh  
- Gourmand, indulgent, yet refined  

**Fragrance Notes**  
- **Top:** Orris, Rice, Bergamot  
- **Heart:** Geranium, Vanilla, Rose  
- **Base:** Ambergris, Pandan, Sandalwood  

**Main Impression**  
- Soft gourmand sweetness  
- Creamy, cozy, and comforting  

**Ideal For**  
- Evening wear  
- Relaxed or intimate settings  
- Customers who enjoy sweet, cozy fragrances  

**Sales Tip**  
"Ondeh Delights is comforting and addictive — sweet like a traditional dessert, but still elegant and wearable."


### D. Packaging
- Each fragrance in the series is presented in a 30 ml bottle
