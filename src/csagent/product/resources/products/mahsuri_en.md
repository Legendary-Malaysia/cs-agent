## 1. Product Overview  
Mahsuri is a floral fragrance inspired by one of Malaysia’s most iconic legends.  

It represents grace, purity, and unwavering courage, translated into a scent that is elegant yet emotionally powerful.  

This fragrance is designed not only to smell beautiful, but to tell a story — making it ideal for customers who value meaning, heritage, and individuality in their perfume choices.  

---

## 2. Core Inspiration & Storytelling  
**The Legend Behind the Fragrance**  
Mahsuri is inspired by the legendary tale of Mahsuri, a symbol of:  
- Grace under adversity  
- Inner strength  
- Purity of spirit  
- Timeless feminine courage  

This story gives the fragrance emotional depth, allowing sales consultants to connect with customers on a cultural and personal level, rather than focusing only on scent notes.  

**Sales Tip**  
When introducing Mahsuri, lead with the story first, then guide the customer into the scent experience.  

**Example Sales Line:**  
“Mahsuri isn’t just a fragrance — it’s inspired by a legendary woman known for her grace, purity, and strength. This perfume captures that spirit in a modern, elegant way.”  

---

## 3. Fragrance Profile  
**Overall Character**  
- **Fragrance Family:** Floral  
- **Mood:** Elegant, graceful, feminine, quietly powerful  
- **Impression:** Soft at first, emotionally memorable, timeless  

**Notes Breakdown**  
- **Top Notes:** Peach, Grapefruit  
  - Creates a fresh, luminous, and gently sweet opening  
- **Heart Notes:** Rose, Muguet  
  - Adds classic floral elegance and femininity  
- **Base Notes:** Musk, Cedarwood  
  - Leaves a clean, warm, and confident trail  

**How to Describe the Scent Simply**  
“It opens fresh and soft, blooms into elegant florals, and settles into a warm, graceful finish.”  

**Available in 30ml bottle**

## 8. Objection Handling Tips  
**Customer: “I prefer something not too strong.”**  
**Response:**  
“Mahsuri is soft and elegant — it doesn’t overpower, but it leaves a beautiful, graceful impression.”  

**Customer: “I want something unique.”**  
**Response:**  
“Mahsuri is inspired by a Malaysian legend, so it carries a story and identity that’s truly distinctive.”

## 9. Key Takeaway

- Emphasize grace, purity, and strength  
- Keep descriptions emotional, not technical  
- Position Mahsuri as a fragrance with soul
