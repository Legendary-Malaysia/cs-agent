1. Product Overview  
Man is a versatile masculine fragrance designed for the modern gentleman—elegant, charismatic, and effortlessly sophisticated.  

It balances fresh citrus brightness, refined florals, and warm woody undertones, creating a confident scent that transitions seamlessly from day to night.  

This fragrance embodies timeless masculinity and is suitable as a signature scent for any occasion.
