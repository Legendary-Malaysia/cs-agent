1. Product Overview  
Violet is a mysterious, elegant, and deeply feminine fragrance that blends floral, fruity, and musky-woody elements into a beautifully layered scent experience. It represents timeless romance, sophistication, and allure—designed for women who embrace both softness and strength.  

This fragrance evolves gracefully on the skin, revealing different layers over time, making it a scent that feels personal, expressive, and unforgettable.  

---

**Story to Share with Customers**

- Luscious femininity

- Effortless elegance

3. Fragrance Profile & Notes  
**Main Character**  
Fruity & Floral with Musky-Woody depth  

**Top Notes (First Impression)**  
- Bergamot  
- Rose  
- Ozone Green  
Creates a fresh, feminine, and uplifting opening.  

**Heart Notes (Core Identity)**  
- Mimosa  
- Magnolia  
- Violet  
- Muguet  
- Iris  
A rich floral bouquet that feels soft, romantic, and irresistibly elegant.  

**Base Notes (Lasting Impression)**  
- Musk  
- Amber  
- Cedar  
- Vetiver  
- Vanilla  
Provides warmth, sensuality, and depth that lingers beautifully on the skin.  

**Available in 30ml bottle**

4. Unique Selling Points (USP)  
**USP 1: Unique, Multi-Layered Scent**  
- A sophisticated blend of floral, fruity, and musky/woody notes  
- The scent evolves over time, revealing new layers the longer it is worn  
- Feels personal and different on every wearer  
**Sales Tip:**  
“Violet is not a one-dimensional perfume—it changes beautifully throughout the day.”  

**USP 2: Long-Lasting Performance**  
- Lasts approximately 4–7 hours  
- Leaves a memorable scent trail without being overpowering  
**Sales Tip:**  
“This is a fragrance that stays with you from day to evening.”  

**USP 3: Easy to Carry**  
- Compact and travel-friendly bottle  
- Perfect for handbags, travel, or touch-ups anytime  
**Sales Tip:**  
“Perfect for women on the go—easy to bring, easy to love.”
