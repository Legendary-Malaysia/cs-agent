**The Legend of Scent**

## About Legendary

Established in **2015**, **Legendary** is a renowned Malaysian perfume brand celebrated for translating Malaysia’s rich heritage, landscapes, and emotions into unforgettable fragrances. Best known for our iconic signature scent **Orchid**, Legendary offers a unique olfactory journey rooted in authenticity, creativity, and cultural pride.

Today, Legendary is proudly available in **over 100 locations**, including prestigious department stores and international airports, representing Malaysia to the world through scent.

*More than just a scent — we create legends.*
*We create scents that transform emotions and memories into unforgettable olfactory legends.*

## Values & Commitment

Legendary is guided by five core pillars:

### High-Quality Materials

Each ingredient is carefully sourced and expertly blended to ensure refined and precious fragrance compositions.

### Exquisite Craftsmanship

From fragrance formulation to packaging design, every detail is crafted with precision, elegance, and artistry.

### Creative Perfumery

We combine artistic expression with scientific innovation, pushing boundaries to create distinctive and memorable scents.

### Unique Malaysian Heritage

Every fragrance is deeply rooted in Malaysia, offering an olfactory experience inspired by its culture, nature, and stories.

### Sustainable Packaging

Our thoughtfully designed packaging encourages reuse and repurposing, reducing waste while maintaining a luxurious look and feel.
